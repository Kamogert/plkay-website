About

PLKAY Studios is a Polokwane-based creative studio providing professional production spaces and creative support for emerging artists and storytellers.

We focus on clean execution, access to quality tools, and building real creative output — not noise.

The studio exists to serve musicians, filmmakers, and creators who want their work to meet professional standards without leaving their city.
